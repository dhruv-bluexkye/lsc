Embracing A Sustainable
Legacy At LSC

At LSC, we embrace sustainability as a core value, integrating it into every facet of our sports and entertainment ventures. We believe in creating a legacy of environmental responsibility, ensuring a greener future for the world of sports.

Our Carbon Neutral Commitment with UCR

In collaboration with the Universal Carbon Registry, LSC is actively engaged in reducing our carbon footprint. Our focused initiatives in carbon offsetting are a testament to our dedication to achieving carbon neutrality in our operations.

Sustainability in Sports Technology

Our commitment to sustainable sports technology is unwavering. From eco-friendly infrastructure to advanced, energy-efficient systems, we're pioneering innovations that protect our planet while enhancing the sports experience.

The Mangrove Initiative:
Guardians of Marine Biodiversity

LSC's mangrove conservation projects are vital in protecting marine ecosystems. These efforts not only preserve the delicate balance of coastal habitats but also serve as a catalyst for marine life rejuvenation.

The biggest risk for our oceans, lakes and rivers is not pollution, it's not plastics, it's not climate change.

The biggest risk is that we give up on the ambition of healthy, abundant water ecosystems.
Adi K Mishra
Founder & CEO

Engaging Communities in Our Green Mission

We believe the path to a sustainable future is a collective effort. At LSC, we engage our fans and communities in meaningful ways, fostering a shared responsibility towards environmental stewardship.

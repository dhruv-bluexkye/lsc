About Us

At League Sports Co (LSC) , we are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of sports entertainment by fusing advanced technologies like Web3, AI, and immersive tech with our investments.

Adi K Mishra

Adi is the Founder and CEO of League Sports Co. Adi K Mishra has been a proponent of early-stage, late-stage, and blockchain-focused technology companies. He maintains a strong passion for sports and using new-age technologies to enhance the fan experience, including fan-based team ownerships. LSC is focused on buying sports rights and introducing elements of esports with a focus on activation through participation for viewers of all age groups. Under his previous role as founder of Karma Innovations and XTZ Esports, he made several investments in technology-linked companies, which later transitioned into a focus on investing in sports and sports technology. Adi is passionate about using technology to scale impact and sustainability initiatives with D-Carbon and environmentally focused sports acquisitions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the sports entertainment landscape by investing in and operating cutting-edge sports tech brands that redefine the boundaries of traditional sports and adapt to the ever-evolving digital era. We aim to create a thriving, interconnected ecosystem across diverse verticals by tapping into the extensive collective experience of our core team, comprised of highly experienced individuals with over 30 years of collective experience in sports and business. Bolstered by a dedicated workforce of 60 professionals, this invaluable knowledge serves as the foundation for driving growth, impact, and success across our portfolio and the industry at large.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a global leader in the sports tech industry by spearheading the convergence of sports, technology, media, and blockchain. We are committed to creating transformative experiences for fans and stakeholders worldwide. Through the integration of advanced technologies like Web3, AI, and immersive tech, we envision a future where sports entertainment is seamlessly interconnected, accessible, and innovative, elevating the industry to new heights and setting the standard for the future of sports tech.

Our Values

At LSC, our core values are the guiding principles that shape our decisions, actions, and culture. They embody our commitment to excellence, innovation, and impact, reflecting our dedication to reshaping the sports tech landscape and creating meaningful experiences for our stakeholders.

Innovation

We relentlessly pursue and embrace cutting-edge technologies and ideas that redefine the boundaries of sports entertainment and create new, immersive experiences for fans.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of synergy, fostering strong partnerships across our diverse portfolio of brands and with industry-leading partners to drive collective growth and impact.

Empowerment

We are committed to empowering fans, athletes, and communities by democratizing sports entertainment through Web3, AI, and blockchain technologies, amplifying their voices and enabling them to actively shape the future.

Sustainability

We recognize our responsibility towards the environment and strive to implement sustainable practices in our operations and investments, driving positive change for a greener, healthier planet.

Inclusivity

We champion emerging sports and untapped markets, fostering an interconnected ecosystem that celebrates diversity, breaks barriers, and creates opportunities for all.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of ethics, transparency, and accountability in all our endeavors, building trust with our stakeholders and ensuring the long-term success of our brands and partners.
